What is Emotinal Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence or EQ is the ability to manage and understand your own emotions, and the emotions of the people around you. Emotional Intelligence is usually associated with empathy, but also related to self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, and social skills.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is about your emotional abilities, social skills and other emotional qualities, how you relate to yourself and others. IQ is about your intelligence, problem solving skills, your cognitive and academic abilities. They both measure your ability to think, but EQ is more about personable skills whereas IQ is about raw intellect.

Why is EQ important?

Having a good sense of your EQ is important because it allows you to manage interpersonal relationships better, have an intrinsic sense of calmness and clarity, and you can work with others in a more productive manner. If you can empathize with others and communicate effectively, you will be an invaluable asset to your team that helps everyone in a concise manner. Feeling heard and valued in a workplace leads to more productivity. Specifically, in a workplace environment where everyone is collaborating on a web project, being able to listen to feedback from an unbiased perspective and understanding others people's wants or needs will help the whole project go smoothly.
